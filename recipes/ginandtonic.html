Classic Gin and Tonic

Nothing hits quite as nice as a classic Gin and Tonic. This lime infused refreshing drink will help your hot summer days melt away

Ingredients

  • 4 cubs of ice
  • 2 fluid ounces gin
  • 4 fluid ounces tonic water/li>
  • 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Place the ice cubes in a tall, narrow glass with the ice coming near the top. Pour gin, tonic water, and lime juice over the ice. Stir well with a long-necked spoon. Garnish with lime wedge, and serve immediately.

  2. Step 2

    Kick back and Enjoy
